# Research Topic Skill

Deep-dives a topic, structures findings, extracts post angles, and saves to the research vault for future content use.

## Inputs
- Topic name or question

## Process
1. Check `research/` for existing files on this topic
2. Research: facts, stats, surprising angles, ZAVINO audience relevance
3. Extract 3–5 post angles with format suggestions
4. Save to `research/{ai-tools|trends|competitors}/YYYY-MM-DD-{slug}.md`
5. Add best angles to `context/backlog.md`

## Use via
`/research [topic]`
