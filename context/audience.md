# ZAVINO — Target Audience

## Primary Audience
Persian-speaking users interested in AI, technology, and marketing.

## Demographics
- **Language:** Persian (Farsi) speakers — Iran, Afghanistan, diaspora
- **Age range:** 18–40
- **Background:** Students, professionals, entrepreneurs, content creators

## Psychographics
- Curious about AI and how it affects their work/life
- Want to stay updated without spending hours reading
- Prefer casual, digestible content over academic articles
- Interested in applying technology practically
- Follow tech trends but may not have deep technical knowledge

## Pain Points
- Too much noise, too little signal in tech news
- Most tech content is in English — hard to access
- Formal/academic Persian content feels distant and dry
- Hard to know which AI tools are actually worth using

## What They Want from ZAVINO
- Quick, clear summaries of what's happening in AI & tech
- Honest takes — not hype, not fear-mongering
- Practical insights they can actually use
- Content that feels like it's from a peer, not a brand

## Audience Segments
| Segment | Description |
|---|---|
| **Curious learners** | Want to understand AI/tech without deep technical knowledge |
| **Professionals** | Using AI in their work, want to stay ahead |
| **Marketers** | Interested in AI for marketing and content creation |
| **Entrepreneurs** | Building or running businesses, want tech edge |
| **Students** | Learning about AI/tech, career-focused |

## Engagement Notes
- Responds well to: questions, polls, relatable observations
- Responds well to: "did you know?" and surprising facts
- Turns off from: obvious recycled news, over-promotion, excessive formal language
