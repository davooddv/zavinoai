# ZAVINO — Content Strategy

## Current Phase
Early growth — building audience, establishing voice, testing content formats.

## Primary Platforms
1. **Instagram** — Visual posts, carousels, reels (main platform)
2. **Twitter/X** — Threads, quick takes, engagement

## Content Mix (Target)
| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| AI tool spotlight | 2x/week | Educate + value |
| Tech/AI news summary | 3x/week | Stay relevant |
| Marketing tips | 1x/week | Broaden appeal |
| Opinion / take | 1x/week | Build personality |
| Engagement post | 1x/week | Community building |

## Content Formats
- **Instagram:** Single image with caption, carousels (3–10 slides), short reels
- **Twitter/X:** Standalone tweets, long threads (5–15 tweets)
- **Newsletter:** Weekly digest of top stories + ZAVINO takes (future)

## Content Principles
1. **Hook first** — lead with the most interesting thing
2. **Short paragraphs** — no walls of text
3. **Practical over theoretical** — "here's how to use it" beats "here's what it is"
4. **Honest opinions** — say when something is overhyped
5. **Consistent voice** — always casual, always clear
